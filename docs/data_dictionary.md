# Data Dictionary

## dim_patient
Synthetic patient dimension with demographics and access-risk proxies.

## fact_referral
Synthetic behavioral health referral facts including created, reviewed, status, priority, and closure fields.

## fact_appointment
Synthetic appointment facts including scheduled date, appointment date, status, type, visit mode, clinic, and provider.

## fact_encounter
Synthetic completed encounter facts linked to completed appointments.

## fact_followup
Synthetic follow-up event facts linked to encounters.

## fact_data_quality_issue
Data quality check outputs with rule id, severity, failed row count, and status.
